# Account Data Management - AddMoney

At **AddMoney**, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. Below you will find the options to manage or delete your information.

### Option 1: Full Account Deletion
If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data (including your email, points balance, and banking information), please follow these steps:

1. Send an email to: **saiegh.saiegh@gmail.com**
2. Use the subject line: **"Full Account Deletion Request"**
3. Include your **User ID** or the **Email address** used to register in the app.

*Note: This action is irreversible. All your transaction history and financial data will be permanently deleted from our Firebase servers within 7 business days.*

---

### Option 2: Partial Data Deletion (Keep Account)
If you want to continue using the app but wish to delete specific information, such as your banking details or transaction history, please follow these steps:

1. Send an email to: **saiegh.saiegh@gmail.com**
2. Use the subject line: **"Partial Data Deletion Request"**
3. Specify which data you want us to remove (e.g., "Delete my banking Alias/CBU" or "Clear my withdrawal history").

*Note: Deleting your banking information will prevent you from requesting new rewards until you provide new payment details.*

---

### Security & Verification
To protect your security, once we receive your email, we will send a verification message to your registered email address to confirm the request before any data is deleted.

If you have any questions, contact us at: **saiegh.saiegh@gmail.com**
