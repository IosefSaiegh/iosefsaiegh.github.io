# Account Deletion Request - AddMoney

At **AddMoney**, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. 

### How to delete your account
If you wish to delete your account and all associated data (including your email, points balance, and banking information), please follow these steps:

1. Send an email to: **saiegh.saiegh@gmail.com**
2. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**
3. Include your **User ID** or the **Email address** used to register in the app.

### What happens after your request?
* Once we receive your email, we will verify your identity.
* All your personal information, including your transaction history and financial data, will be permanently deleted from our Firebase servers within **7 business days**.
* Please note that this action is irreversible.
