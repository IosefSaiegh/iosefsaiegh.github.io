# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Add Money app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Iosef Saiegh (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

**Information Collection and Use**

For a better experience, while using the Application, the Service Provider may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:
* **User Identity:** Email, userId, and Name.
* **Financial Information:** To process your reward withdrawals and payments, the Application collects banking details provided voluntarily by you, such as Account Number, CBU/CVU, Alias, or Virtual Wallet details.

The Application also collects information automatically when you download and use it, including:
* Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address).
* The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and time spent on those pages.
* The operating system you use on your mobile device.

**Financial Data Handling**

The Service Provider uses your financial and banking data **strictly and exclusively** to fulfill payment requests and reward redemptions initiated by you. 
* This data is processed securely through Firebase encrypted databases.
* The Service Provider does not share your specific banking details with third-party marketing companies.

**Location Data**

The Application collects your device's location to help the Service Provider determine your approximate geographical location to be used for:
* **Geolocation Services:** Providing personalized content and relevant recommendations.
* **Analytics and Improvements:** Analyzing user behavior and trends to improve Application performance.

**Data Retention Policy**

The Service Provider will retain User Provided data (including financial information) for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter to comply with payment records. If you'd like them to delete User Provided Data, please contact them at **saiegh.saiegh@gmail.com**.

**Children**

The Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. If a child under 13 has provided personal or financial information, it will be immediately deleted from the servers.

**Security**

The Service Provider provides physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards (including HTTPS encryption via Firebase) to protect the confidentiality of your personal and financial information.
